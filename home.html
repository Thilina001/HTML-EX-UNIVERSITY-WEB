Upcoming Events


MoU was signed in between Honghe University, Mengzi, China and the Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka.

The Memorandum of Understanding between Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka and Honghe University was signed on 28th November 2016 at Honghe University, Mengzi, China. The Vice Chancellor of Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka, Dean of the faculty of Social Sciences and Languages and Coordinator of the Joint Academic Programme participated the event from Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka. Future academic corporation, exchange of students and staff members and establishment of Sinhala Language courses and programmes at Honghe University, China were agreed upon. Another fruitful discussion followed the event where a joint academic programme of Chinese Language was further discussed.
